	# Project Proposal Source Pack

## Purpose

This pack separates the currently attached Zotero full-text papers into proposal-writing roles so you can:

- upload a smaller, better-scoped set of PDFs when drafting
- cite the right papers for the right section
- avoid overusing broad context papers as if they were direct device precedents

## Best Upload Order For Proposal Drafting

If you want a minimal high-value upload set first, start with these papers:

1. `Evolving concepts on bradykinesia` (Bologna et al., 2020)
2. `Analyzing the ‘Bradykinesia Complex’ in Parkinson's Disease` (Paparella et al., 2026)
3. `Computer Vision for Parkinson’s Disease Evaluation: A Survey on Finger Tapping` (Amo-Salas et al., 2024)
4. `Interpretable and granular video-based quantification of motor characteristics from the finger-tapping test in Parkinson’s disease` (Zarrat Ehsan et al., 2026)
5. `Design and Validation of a Self-Aligning Index Finger Exoskeleton for Post-Stroke Rehabilitation` (Sun et al., 2021)
6. `Self-Aligning Finger Exoskeleton for the Mobilization of the Metacarpophalangeal Joint` (Peperoni et al., 2023)
7. `A hand exoskeleton with linear motors (HELM) for pathological tremor suppression of fingers` (Yi et al., 2025)
8. `Predicting the response of a tendon-driven prosthetic finger with hyperelastic joints` (O'Toole, 2025)

Add these next if you need Parkinson-specific rehab positioning:

1. `Improving Upper Extremity Bradykinesia in Parkinson’s Disease: A Randomized Clinical Trial on the Use of Gravity-Supporting Exoskeletons` (Raciti et al., 2022)
2. `Exploring the impact of robotic hand rehabilitation on functional recovery in parkinson’s disease: a randomized controlled trial` (Raciti et al., 2025)
3. `iTex Gloves: Design and In-Home Evaluation of an E-Textile Glove System for Tele-Assessment of Parkinson’s Disease` (Ravichandran et al., 2023)

## Short Recommendation

For a thesis proposal, your citation backbone should probably be:

- `background/problem`: Bologna 2020, Paparella 2026
- `task-definition/measurement`: Amo-Salas 2024, Zarrat Ehsan 2026, Ravichandran 2023
- `direct hardware precedent`: Sun 2021, Peperoni 2023, Yi 2025
- `modeling/method choice`: O'Toole 2025, Sun 2021, Peperoni 2023
- `Parkinson rehab positioning`: Raciti 2022 and optionally Raciti 2025

## Important Use Rule

Do not let `pinch` papers or `broad Parkinson context` papers become your main mechanical justification unless your proposal explicitly pivots in that direction.

The strongest direct bridge to the current thesis scope still looks like:

- finger-tapping and bradykinesia papers for `why this task matters`
- self-aligning finger/MCP exoskeleton papers for `how a simplified mechanism can be designed`
- reduced-order tendon/joint modeling papers for `why a simple interpretable model is defensible`
