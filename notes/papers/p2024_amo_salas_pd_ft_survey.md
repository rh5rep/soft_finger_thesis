## Abstract / Summary

- This is a review paper focused specifically on computer-vision approaches for Parkinson finger-tapping evaluation.
- It is useful because it turns the assessment side of the literature into a taxonomy rather than a scattered list of one-off systems.
- The paper helps define the evaluation problem and clarifies that direct tapping-task literature is rich in sensing and analysis, even if it is sparse in exoskeleton hardware.
- For your review, this paper should anchor the `assessment track` rather than the hardware track.
